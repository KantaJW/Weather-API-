About Us

With the vision of making everyone’s outdoor activities more planned, can one go outside and surf, is it enough wind? Can one work on a sunburn or is it one of those days of Netflix and chill, again?

WeatherChannel is here for you, to make you know when to put on the raincoat to put on some slippers and go out and have a picnic in the park. We’re here to make it more easy for you to plan your day, what shoes to wear or do you really need that umbrella for a walk in the city?

We wanted to make it possible to get easy access to the news of the weather anywhere the world so that’s why WeatherChannel was formed, 2021 in February by a team of five. We’re here for you, and hope you’ll like our site and all that comes with! If you still have any questions you can just Contact us!
